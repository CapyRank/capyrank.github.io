How it works

01
Streamers join CapyRank
Connect your Twitch account, then type /mod capyrank in your chat to give the bot the access it needs. Takes about 30 seconds.
02
Viewers give Capys daily
Every viewer gets 100 Capys per day to spend across any connected channel. Type !capy in chat — or !capy 10 to give more at once.
03
Climb your league
Capys earned move you up the leaderboard within your follower league. Small streamers compete on fair ground.
04
Get discovered
Your profile page shows your ranking, top supporters, and live status. Share it anywhere — it's your community's vote of confidence.
