Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about CapyRank.

General
CapyRank is a community-driven streamer discovery platform. Viewers give daily Capys to streamers they enjoy watching, and those Capys move streamers up a leaderboard within their follower league. It's a way for small streamers to get discovered based on community support rather than raw viewer counts.
Nope — CapyRank is completely free for both streamers and viewers.
Viewers don't need to sign up for anything — just type !capy in any CapyRank-connected channel and you're good to go. Streamers need to connect their Twitch account via the Join CapyRank flow, which takes about 30 seconds.
For Viewers
Type !capy in the chat of any CapyRank-connected streamer to give 1 Capy. Want to give more? Type !capy 10 (or any number) to give multiple at once.
Every viewer gets 100 Capys per day to spend however they like — across one channel or many. Your allowance resets at midnight UTC each day.

💛 Want more? Follow CapyRank on Twitch to unlock 150 Capys per day — an extra 50 to spread the love!

For Streamers
Click Join CapyRank, connect your Twitch account, then type /mod capyrank in your Twitch chat to give the bot the access it needs. That's it — your profile page goes live immediately.
Twitch requires the bot to have moderator status in your channel in order to read your follower count, which is used to place you in the correct league. The bot won't moderate your chat or take any actions beyond tracking Capys.
!capy — give 1 Capy to the streamer

!capy 10 — give multiple Capys at once

!capys — check your remaining daily allowance

!capyrank — see the streamer's current rank

!capytop — show the top supporters of the channel
Leagues are based on your Twitch follower count, so you compete on a level playing field with streamers of a similar size.
